Making You Well Prepared & Well Protected

Melinium Taekwondo and Karate Academy, located in Ghana, has gained recognition for its welcoming and inclusive approach to martial arts instruction. Since its establishment in 2018, our academy has successfully trained over 200 international students, drawing participants from Europe and various African countries, all of whom have excelled in kung fu and karate. Set against the backdrop of picturesque mountains, our academy offers a comfortable and motivating environment for personal development.
